Vision And Positioning

FirstUser turns passive waitlists into active communities where founders drive pre-launch momentum and early users earn recognition for participation.

Personas And Product Scope (As-Is)

Founder

Needs a structured way to convert anonymous waitlist signups into active members, then moderate and activate the community before launch.

Success: approved active members, productive conversations, lower manual moderation effort.

Early User

Wants proof of being early, access to founders, and rewards for real participation instead of waiting silently on a list.

Success: verified join, badge progression, repeat participation.

Platform Admin

Needs a lightweight control loop to collect ideas, prioritize improvements, and keep product quality high.

Success: healthy idea backlog and clear prioritization cycles.

Implemented product modules
  • Acquisition and discovery surfaces
  • Phone and email OTP auth, onboarding, username/profile setup
  • CreateSpace wizard with drafts, upload, and image crop flow
  • Waitlist positioning, member status lifecycle, founder moderation
  • Community channels, direct messages, reactions, search, unread state
  • Founder tools: announcements, polls, custom badges, stats
  • In-app notifications and admin ideas backlog
  • Socket-based realtime presence, typing, and message delivery
Out of scope in current implementation
  • No dedicated in-app browser error screenshot reporting workflow
  • No full production observability runbook in repo
  • No broad automated critical-path test suite yet
  • No distributed OTP store or queue-backed high-scale delivery architecture

User Journey Narratives

New user to verified participant

1
Discover: User lands on public pages and explores AppSpaces.
2
Verify: User authenticates by phone or email OTP.
3
Join: User enters waitlist and gets position plus status context.
4
Engage: Approved users move into active community participation.

Founder setup to active moderation

1
Create: Founder runs CreateSpace flow and publishes.
2
Moderate: Founder approves/rejects members (single and bulk).
3
Activate: Founder publishes announcements, polls, and badge awards.
4
Optimize: Founder uses stats and feedback loops to iterate.

Risks, Gaps, And Completion Roadmap

Current launch blockers are known and mapped into a phased completion plan.

Phase 1 Launch Readiness

  • Move OTP state to durable shared storage.
  • Clarify SMS provider strategy and remove ambiguity.
  • Add critical path tests (auth, moderation, messaging).
  • Add baseline observability and incident triage flow.
  • Harden permission checks and abuse protections.

Phase 2 Growth

  • Instrument funnel drop-offs and onboarding conversion points.
  • Improve founder activation workflows and targeting options.
  • Extend engagement loops with smarter notifications.
  • Introduce analytics views for founder decisions.

Phase 3 Scale Hardening

  • Improve realtime and messaging throughput resilience.
  • Add stronger operational safeguards and capacity checks.
  • Formalize reliability objectives and runbooks.
  • Reduce platform risk before larger traffic expansion.

Launch readiness checklist

  • Security and auth hardening complete
  • Core UX reliability verified across key routes
  • Data integrity and moderation safeguards validated
  • Observability and error triage baseline in place

Success metrics

  • North star: approved-to-active community conversion
  • Activation rate and verified join rate
  • D30 user retention
  • Founder management throughput
  • Announcement and notification engagement
